Calculation Assumptions
This calculator uses the following assumptions to model your AI ROI. Please review them carefully before proceeding.
ℹ️
Why this matters Every financial model rests on assumptions. Understanding these helps you interpret the results in the right context and adjust inputs for a more accurate estimate.
👥
Uniform Productivity Distribution
Productivity gains are applied uniformly across all impacted employees. Individual variation in adoption speed or skill levels is not modelled.
🤖
20% Labour Cost is Automation-Addressable
A conservative 20% of total labour cost is treated as candidates for full automation. This reflects industry benchmarks for knowledge-work automation.
📅
Benefits Are Prorated in Year 1
Only the months remaining after the stated go-live date in Year 1 generate benefits. Pre-deployment phases contribute zero return.
🏢
Stable Headcount Over Projection Period
Employee headcount remains constant throughout the selected projection horizon. Neither growth nor attrition is factored into benefit scaling.
💵
Costs Incurred As Planned – No Overruns
Implementation and licensing costs match your inputs exactly. Budget overruns, contract renegotiations, or hidden costs are not included.
🌍
Industry Multiplier Applied Uniformly
The selected industry vertical applies a single multiplier derived from published sector AI-ROI benchmarks. Sub-sector variances are not captured.
📉
Error Reduction Is Linear
The error/rework reduction percentage is applied directly and linearly to your declared annual rework cost baseline with no diminishing returns.
💱
All Values in INR (₹) – No FX Adjustment
Calculations assume Indian Rupees (₹) throughout. Multi-currency organisations should convert all values to INR equivalents before entering inputs.
I have read and understand all stated assumptions

Risks That May Impact Results
These are real-world factors that could reduce your actual ROI below the projected figure. Accepting these risks means you acknowledge they exist.
⚡
Important DisclaimerThis tool provides indicative estimates only. Results are not a guarantee of financial returns. Engage a qualified AI advisor for a detailed business case.
🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Change Resistance HIGH
Employees resisting AI adoption is the #1 cause of failed deployments. Insufficient change management can reduce effective productivity gains by 40–60%.
🗃️
Data Quality Issues HIGH
Poor, incomplete, or siloed data can delay go-live and significantly degrade AI model performance, reducing accuracy and automation coverage.
📈
Implementation Cost Overruns HIGH
Enterprise AI projects frequently experience 20–40% cost overruns due to scope creep, integration complexity, and underestimated change management effort.
⚖️
Regulatory & Compliance Changes MEDIUM
Evolving AI regulations (EU AI Act, GDPR, sector-specific rules) may require costly redesign, re-auditing, or even suspension of AI initiatives.
🔒
Vendor Lock-In & Price Escalation MEDIUM
Platform licensing costs often increase at renewal. Switching costs may be high, reducing the flexibility to optimise spend over the projection period.
🔗
Legacy System Integration Complexity MEDIUM
Integrating AI with legacy applications typically extends time-to-value by 2–6 months, delaying the benefit realisation shown in Year 1 projections.
🧠
AI Model Drift Over Time MEDIUM
AI models degrade in accuracy as data distributions shift. Without continuous monitoring and retraining, efficiency gains can erode within 12–18 months.
🛡️
Cybersecurity & Data Privacy Exposure MEDIUM
AI systems introduce new attack surfaces. A breach or data-leak incident could trigger regulatory penalties and reputational costs not captured in this model.
⏳
ROI Realisation Lag LOW–MED
Even after go-live, full operational benefits typically materialise 3–9 months later as teams build confidence and refine workflows.
👩‍💻
AI Talent & Skills Gap LOW
Shortage of qualified AI/ML engineers and prompt engineers could slow deployment or require higher-than-budgeted external consulting spend.
I acknowledge these risks and understand results are indicative estimates only
